Cheeseburger

A delicious and well grilled cheeseburger. Topped with lettuce as well as the basic ingredients of: Buns, cheese, and meat

Ingredients

  • 1.5 Pounds ground beef (80% to 85% lean
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 to 6 hamburger buns, split
  • Butter or oil, for the pan
  • 4 to 6 slices of cheese
  • (Optional toppings):Tomatoes, onions, lettuce, ketchup, etc

Steps to create

  1. Divide the ground beef
  2. Shape the patties
  3. Warn the pan to medium heat
  4. Toast the buns
  5. Increase heat to medium high and place the patties on the pan
  6. Cook the burgers for 3 to 5 minutes and genersously season
  7. Flip the burgers and cook another 3 to 5 minutes
  8. Top with cheese as soon as you flip the patties
  9. All done! Apply any toppings to your liking!
